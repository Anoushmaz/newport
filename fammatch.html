In 2018, Leeuwarden was the cultural capital city (Shared with Valleta, Malta) of Europe. For this, I had to define a concept that will bring international families together to the city.

With the FAMMATCH app, two families can be matched together by their interests. These interests have been merged and converted into an educational quest through the city. The families can walk this path together and maybe getting a friendship for life or otherwise an interesting experience. For the research and prototype, please download the PDF.

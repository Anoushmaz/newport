MELKWEG poster

Visual Design, 2018

MELKWEG is a concert hall in Amsterdam. For this assignment I had to make a calendar poster. The challenge was to fit all the text on just one A3 sized poster.

The other poster is a poster of one show. Here, the challenge was to make a poster show a feeling (of the sort music they make) with only text. You couldn't use any visuals like blocks or shapes. Just letters.

Zenza

Project Sleep, 2018

We sleep for one third of our lives. Some people have issues trying to fall a sleep. For example, nightworkers in hospitals. They work in the night and try to sleep in the morning. The sleeping pattern for a nightworker ruined by this. Therefor, we made an interactive solution.

Zenza is a little light in the shape of a litte tree that you can place on your nightstand. It's connected with an app which will tell you excactly when to sleep and when to wake up. The tree helps with a dimming and waking up light. For all the details and design choices, please download the PDF.

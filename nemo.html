NEMO logo design

Visual Design, 2018

NEMO is a science museum in Amsterdam made for children. They already have a beautiful logo for their company, but for this project we needed to make a re-design.

I started my process by visiting the museum myself. I made pictures and was observing everything I saw. Then, I brainstormed after looking at the feeling that the children have in the museum and how everyone behaved including the parents. In the end, I made two re-designs as you can see at the picture.

Hi! My name is Anoush Mazloumian, I'm 21 and living in a small city called Medemblik (40 km north of Amsterdam).

I'm studying Communication & Multimedia Design at the University of Applied Sciences in Amsterdam.

Here, I've learned everything about user and human centered design. Especially for interactive products, like mobile interfaces.

In 2020, I did an UX/UI design internship at M2Mobi, an award winning full-service mobile agency based in Amsterdam.

In my personal life, I like spending time outside, looking up the crowds and bingewatch netflix shows.

Family is very important to me. This is my sister and me when I visited her while she was doing her internship in Spain.

In 2019 I stayed two months in Canada visiting my Armenian family. I love to travel, meet new people and go on adventures.

I hope you now have some kind of an idea of who I am. Please see my skills for an overview of my knowledge and abilities in Design.

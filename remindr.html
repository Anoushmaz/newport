Project Join The Pipe, 2019

Join The Pipe is a company who sells bottles to decrease the plastic waste of diposable bottles.

There is only one important problem that comes with this Join The Pipe bottles. People easily forget to take the Join The Pipe bottle with them or they lose it quickly, after they still will buy a disposable bottle. For this problem, we made a solution. A cap that you can put on your bottle that is connected to an app. The app shows exactly where your bottle is located if you have lost it. The app also lets you know in time if you are in the situation to forget the bottle. You can purchase the cap voluntarily, which makes it a feasible and innovative suitable solution.

The Texack

Behavorial Design, 2019

Every month, people receive a textile bag through the letterbox. This bag, also known as the Texack, is connected to an app that states exactly what should be in the bag, when the bags are collected and how well the neighborhood is recycling textile in relation to other neighborhoods.

Every month there is a different clothing category. Sometimes these can be socks and other times, for example, jeans. In this way it is better for the upcycling clothing factories and the tasks for the user are divided into small steps, so that the user will do this sooner.

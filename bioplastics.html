Bioplastics

Minor Makerslab, 2020

At the minor Makerslab I learned everything about the essentials of critical making and collaborative learning. I had the oppertunity to work with laser cutters, RISO printers, 3D printing and modelling and how to make my own bioplastic which I can use in an innovative way.

Because the Corona virus hitted our world after a month of this minor, I couldn't use the machines anymore. So I specialised in making my own, super easy bioplastics. Experimenting, testing, researching, experimenting, testing, researching, experimenting etc. was my way of doing. In the documentation below you can see the results of this process.

Bioplastics

Minor Makerslab, 2020

At the minor Makerslab I learned everything about the essentials 'critical making'. I had the oppertunity to work with laser cutters, RISO printers, 3D printing and modelling and how to make my own bioplastics which I can use in many different innovative ways.

Because the Corona virus hitted our world after a month of this minor, I couldn't use the machines anymore. So I specialised in making my own bioplastics. In the link below, you can find the full documentation of the process.

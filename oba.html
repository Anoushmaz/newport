OBA

Visual Interface Design, 2017

OBA stands for Openbare (open) Bibliotheek (library) Amsterdam. They started a project that kids will easily rent books and find what they like. I chose to focus on kids with the age around 12 and designed a app for them where they can easily reserve a book what fits their interest and pick it up from the nearest location from where they are at that time.

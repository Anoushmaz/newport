Scheepsvaartmuseum re-design

Mini-project, 2019

This mini-project is based on increasing the conversion ratio of a website. I learned everything about A/B testing and conversion. Unfortunately, we did not put this into real practice with our project.

The project was to make a webdesign for a specific user group of the Scheepsvaartmuseum. With a special computer program we praticed and tested our wireframes. The program can exactly indicate where the user is watching first and second on the website. We took these insights into our further designs. For all the wireframes, download the PDF.
